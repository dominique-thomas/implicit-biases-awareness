About

The purpose of this training is to increase awareness of implicit or unconscious biases to better promote inclusivity and empathy towards others. This training is designed to be completed a self-paced corporate environment; however, it can also serve as a supplementary tool in an instructor-led environment. The training content is provided as-is and free to use.

The website, course content, videos, and resources were designed and developed by Dominique Thomas.

The last update for this training was made on 06/03/2024.
